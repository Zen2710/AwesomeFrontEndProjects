COVID-19
Stay Home Stay Safe

Stay home stay safe. Stay physically fit. Exercise regulary. Eat a nutritious diet. Don’t smoke.If any member of the household shows symptoms of Covid-19, seek medical advice and follow your local health authority's guidance.

Protection

Protection 1

Wear A Face Mask

Make sure you wear mask when you go outside. Also make it compulsory for everyone who visits your house.

Protection 2

Wash Your Hands

Keep a sanitizer or handwash at the entrance of your home. Make it compulsory to sanitize for everyone who visits your house.

Protection 3

Avoid Close Contact

Practice Social Distancing.Maintain Minimum of 6ft distance between you and others.

Symptoms

SYMPTOM 1

Fever : Keep Monitoring Your Temperature Twice A Day And Is Temperature is Abnormal,Get Medical Attention.

SYMPTOM 2

Sore Throat : If you feel itchiness is your throat,do not neglect it and take necessary medication.

SYMPTOM 3

Tiredness : If you are feeling tired without reason than first take rest and if persists do a checkup.

SYMPTOM 4

Aches and Pain : Aches and Pain Go Side To Side With Fever and Tiredness and are very painful.

SYMPTOM 5

Shortness of Breath : If you experience shortness of breath,monitor your oxygen level and if it is less than 93 than get attention immediately.

How to get Vaccinated

[1] To register yourself on Cowin website go to https://selfregistration.cowin.gov.in/

[2] Sign for vaccination by entering your phone number in the given column.

[3] The website will send you an OTP to verify, you can also login using your Aarogya Setu account.

[4] Tap on Register for the vaccine. Select your photo ID proof— it could be your Aadhar Card, Voters ID, Driving License.

[5] Enter the Photo ID number, select your name and specify your gender and mention your year of birth.

[6] After filling in all the details, tap on Register.

[7] Once you do that, you will be asked to pick your nearest vaccination center.

[8] Select your time slot based on the availability. You can either chose the morning or afternoon slot.

[9] You will get a message on your registered number when you successfully book a slot.

[10] Do not forget to carry your Photo ID like Aadhar to the vaccination center.
